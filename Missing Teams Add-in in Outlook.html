Missing Teams Add-in in Outlook

Overview

This guide is intended to assist users in restoring the Microsoft Teams Add-in if it is missing from Outlook.

Procedure

  1. Close Outlook completely.
  2. Locate the Teams icon in your system tray (near the date and time).
    • Right-click the icon and select “Quit.”

  3. Reopen Microsoft Teams.
  4. In the top-right corner of Teams, click your profile icon and select "Sign out."
  5. Once signed out, sign back into Teams.
  6. Send a message to a colleague or contact via Teams.
  7. Open Outlook again.
  8. The Teams Add-in should now appear in Outlook.
  9. If the issue persists, please contact the Service Desk for further assistance.
